VLC is a free and open source cross-platform multimedia player and framework that plays most multimedia files as well as DVD, Audio CD, VCD, and various streaming protocols.

Simple, fast and powerful media player. No spyware, no ads, no user tracking. Join our community of 500 million monthly users and enjoy unrestricted playback of your favorite media.

Simple, fast and powerful

  • Plays everything: Files, Discs, Webcams, Devices and Streams
  • Plays most codecs with no codec packs needed: MPEG-2, MPEG-4, H.264, MKV, WebM, WMV, MP3...
  • Runs on all platforms: Windows, Linux, Mac OS X, Unix, iOS, Android...
  • Completely Free: no spyware, no ads and no user tracking
  • Convert, compress, download subtitles, and more advanced features for power users
  • Low resource usage, hardware acceleration support, and customizable interface
